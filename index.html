Who am I

About me


I am a reporter in the Chinese blockchain industry.
China has always been a land of mystery--it says “no” to Bitcoin, but “yes” to the blockchain technology.
China shut down crypto exchanges, cracked down initial coin offerings, banned bitcoin mining operations, which is often interpreted the beginning of an all-out war against the crypto economy.
Is China truly taking every move to destroy Bitcoin? What is the current status of bitcoin mining companies under administration? How are industrial players doing in China?
If you ever expect an answer, if you ever feel curious about China, follow Cindydaily.
I chose to share what is going on in the Chinese community at 8BTC and Bitcoin.com and now write on Yours.org to promote the use of Bitcoin Cash.
I will continue to write China-related stories for the rest of my life.
